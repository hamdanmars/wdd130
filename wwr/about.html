At White Water Rafting, our mission is to bring adventure and excitement to life! Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned rafter, we've got the perfect thrill for you. Safety, joy, and unforgettable memories are what we promise!

History

Founded in 2002, White Water Rafting Co. started with a single raft and a big dream. From that first wild ride with a handful of friends, we’ve grown into a top-rated adventure company known for scenic routes, expert guides, and unforgettable memories.

White Water Rafting. launched on 25th May 2002 with nothing more than one raft, a river map, and a passion for adventure. What began as weekend getaways for friends quickly turned into a full-fledged rafting company. Over the years, we’ve carved out a reputation for heart-pounding rapids, unbeatable views, and authentic river experiences. Today, we guide thousands of adventurers each season but we still remember every twist, every turn, and every splash that got us here.
